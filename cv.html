Academic Career Summary

I am a Social Anthropologist currently working on a book manuscripts provisionally titled ‘Urban Informality in the Twenty-First Century India: Politics of the Poor at edges’. I was an ICSSR Fellow in the Centre for Studies in Social Sciences, Calcutta. I worked as a Post Doctoral Fellow in the department of Cross-Cultural and Regional Studies, University of Copenhagen, Denmark. I taught sociology at the University of Burdwan and Jadavpur University before joining the University of Helsinki, Finland. I also worked also as a senior researcher at the CSSSC, Calcutta, and Pratichi (India) Trust.

Award

  • 2022 - Awarded a Grant for conducting research as part of the project entitled ‘Sustainable Livelihoods and the Politics at the Margins: Environmental Displacement in South Asia’ by the department of History and Ethnology, University of Jyvaskyla, Finland.
  • 2021 - Awarded a Grant for writing a book in Bengali as part of the research project titled ‘Sustainable Livelihoods and the Politics at the Margins: Environmental Displacement in South Asia’ by Helsinki Institute of Urban and Regional Studies (URBARIA).
  • 2018 - Awarded a Grant for a collaborative research project titled ‘Sustainable Livelihoods and the Politics at the Margins: Environmental Displacement in South Asia’ by the Academy of Finland.
  • 2015 - Awarded a book writing grant for the book manuscript titled, ‘Employment, Rights and Hegemony in Neo-liberal India’ by ERC, ICSSR.
  • 2009 - Awarded a Post Doctoral Fellowship in the University of Copenhagen for a collaborative research programme entitled Nation in Motion: Globalization, Development and Governance in India by the Danish Agency for Science and Higher Education, Copenhagen, Denmark.
  • 2008 - Awarded a Guest PhD Fellowship in Roskilde University, Denmark for three months
  • 2005 - Awarded the ICSSR Doctoral Fellowship in Political Sociology at the Centre for Studies in Social Sciences, Calcutta
